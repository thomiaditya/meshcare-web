What is Psychology??

All Kinds Psychology

  • General and Experimental Psychology

    General and Experimental Psychology, we learn about research methodologies, respondents, psychological measurement tools, and much more

    Last updated 3 mins ago
    Clinical Psychology

    In Clinical Psychology, we study human behavior in more detail. A Clinical Psychologist treats a variety of individual mental conditions, ranging from mild such as overcoming a crisis of confidence to those that are categorized as severe such as psychopathic behavior.

    Last updated 3 mins ago
    Developmental Psychology

    Our mental development from childhood to old age undergoes interesting stages of process. Each stage is studied scientifically in Developmental Psychology. So it is in this field of Psychology that we learn about adolescent development, children's education, and so on.

    Last updated 3 mins ago
    Educational Psychology

    Educational Psychology also looks at mental capacity at a certain age. The difference is, Educational Psychology focuses more on educational approaches and methods that are appropriate for a certain age and population

    Last updated 3 mins ago
    Industrial and Organizational Psychology

    The focus of Industrial and Organizational Psychology is to maintain the interaction and psychological condition of everyone in the company in a prime condition so that their work productivity is maximized. The screening and recruitment system for prospective employees is also studied here

    Last updated 3 mins ago
    Social Psychology

    The field of study in Social Psychology includes conflict resolution, mass psychology, and many more. Have you ever seen a mass demonstration that was originally calm can turn into a riot? Social Psychology Analysis can explain this phenomenon. In fact, Social Psychology is useful for regulating the masses and preventing the riots

    Last updated 3 mins ago
